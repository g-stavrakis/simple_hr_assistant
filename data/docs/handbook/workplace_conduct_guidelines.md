# Workplace Conduct Guidelines

## Respectful Behavior
Employees are expected to treat colleagues, clients, and partners with professionalism and respect in all work settings.
Harassment, bullying, intimidation, and discriminatory conduct are not permitted.

## Conflicts of Interest
Employees must disclose any outside activity, financial interest, or personal relationship that could influence business decisions.
Managers and HR will review disclosures and determine whether mitigation steps are required.

## Reporting Concerns
Employees should report workplace conduct concerns to their manager, HR, or an approved ethics reporting channel.
Retaliation against anyone who raises a concern in good faith is prohibited.
