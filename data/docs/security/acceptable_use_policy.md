# Acceptable Use Policy

## Company Systems
Company systems and network resources must be used primarily for legitimate business purposes.
Limited personal use is acceptable when it is occasional, lawful, and does not interfere with work responsibilities.

## Restricted Activities
Employees must not install unauthorized software, disable security controls, or access data outside their job responsibilities.
Using company systems to store or transmit offensive, illegal, or unlicensed material is prohibited.

## Monitoring and Enforcement
The company may monitor system usage, logs, and security events in accordance with applicable law and internal controls.
Violations of this policy may result in removal of access, disciplinary action, or legal escalation.
