# Parental Leave Policy

## Primary Caregiver Leave
Employees designated as the primary caregiver are eligible for up to 16 weeks of paid parental leave.
This leave must be taken within 12 months of the child's birth, adoption, or foster placement.

## Secondary Caregiver Leave
Employees designated as the secondary caregiver are eligible for up to 6 weeks of paid parental leave.
Leave may be taken continuously or in two separate blocks with manager and HR approval.

## Notice Period
Employees should notify their manager and HR at least 30 days before the planned start of parental leave when feasible.
HR will provide documentation requirements and benefit continuation details during the planning process.
