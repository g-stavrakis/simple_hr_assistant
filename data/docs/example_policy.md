# Employee Handbook

## Probation Period
All new employees are subject to a probation period of 3 months starting from their official date of employment.
During this period, managers conduct at least one formal check-in to review progress, expectations, and any support needs.

## Annual Leave
Employees receive 25 working days of annual leave per calendar year.
Unused leave may be carried over for up to 5 days into the next calendar year, subject to manager approval.

## Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Teams may define core collaboration days for in-person meetings.
