# Business Travel Policy

## Booking Rules
Employees must book business travel through the approved corporate travel platform whenever possible.
Flights should be booked at least 14 days in advance unless there is a documented business need for last-minute travel.

## Hotel Limits
Hotel bookings should not exceed 180 EUR per night before taxes in standard travel locations.
Higher rates require manager approval and should be justified by event pricing, safety, or limited availability.

## Meal Reimbursement
Employees may expense up to 60 EUR per day for meals while traveling overnight for work.
Alcohol is not reimbursable unless it is part of an approved client dinner.
