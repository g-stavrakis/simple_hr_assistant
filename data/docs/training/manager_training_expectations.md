# Manager Training Expectations

## Required Manager Courses
People managers must complete training on interviewing, feedback, performance management, and legal basics within 60 days of becoming a manager.
Refresher training may be assigned when company policy or employment law requirements change.

## Participation Standards
Managers are expected to attend live workshops when required and complete any pre-work or follow-up activities by the assigned deadline.
Repeated failure to complete manager training may affect eligibility for future people leadership responsibilities.

## Team Development Role
Managers should identify development opportunities for team members during career conversations and performance reviews.
Training requests should be prioritized when they address critical skill gaps or planned business needs.
