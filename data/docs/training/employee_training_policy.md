# Employee Training and Development Policy

## Mandatory Training
All employees must complete information security, code of conduct, and data privacy training within 14 days of their start date.
Required refresher courses must be completed annually by the deadline assigned in the learning system.

## Professional Development
Employees may request manager approval for external courses, certifications, workshops, or conferences that support business and role goals.
Approved training should align with the employee's development plan and department priorities.

## Tracking and Completion
Managers are responsible for reviewing training completion status during regular one-on-ones.
Employees who miss mandatory training deadlines may have system access restricted until overdue courses are completed.
