# Corporate Card Policy

## Card Usage
Corporate cards may only be used for approved business expenses and must never be used for personal purchases.
Cardholders are responsible for reviewing transactions promptly and reporting any suspicious activity to Finance.

## Monthly Reconciliation
Employees must reconcile corporate card charges in the expense system within 10 business days of the monthly statement close.
Missing receipts or delayed reconciliation may result in temporary suspension of card privileges.

## Lost or Stolen Cards
Lost or stolen cards must be reported to the card issuer immediately and to Finance on the same business day.
Employees must document any unauthorized charges and cooperate with the dispute process.
