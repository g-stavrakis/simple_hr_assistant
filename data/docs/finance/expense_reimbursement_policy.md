# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Submission Deadline
Employees must submit expense reports within 30 days of the purchase date.
Reports submitted after 30 days may be denied unless the delay is approved by Finance.

## Receipt Requirements
Receipts are required for any individual expense above 25 EUR.
If a receipt is unavailable, the employee must provide a written explanation and manager approval.

## Approval Workflow
All expense reports require approval from the employee's direct manager before reimbursement is processed.
Approved reimbursements are typically paid in the next payroll cycle.
