# Sick Leave Policy

## Sick Leave Entitlement
Employees are eligible for up to 10 paid sick days per calendar year for personal illness, medical appointments, or short-term recovery needs.
Part-time employee entitlements are prorated based on their standard working schedule.

## Notification Expectations
Employees should notify their manager as soon as reasonably possible when they are unable to work because of illness.
If an absence extends beyond 3 consecutive working days, HR may request a medical certificate where permitted by law.

## Misuse and Support
Managers should partner with HR if absence patterns suggest misuse or if an employee needs workplace accommodations.
The company will handle health-related information confidentially and limit access to those with a business need to know.
