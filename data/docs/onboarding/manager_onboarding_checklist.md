# Manager Onboarding Checklist

## Pre-Start Preparation
Managers must confirm role responsibilities, a 90-day plan, and required system access before the employee's first day.
New hires should have a named onboarding buddy and a prepared first-week schedule before they join.

## First Month Responsibilities
Managers are expected to review goals, key stakeholders, and team norms during the first two weeks.
At least one shadowing or knowledge-transfer session should be arranged for each core responsibility in the employee's role.

## Escalation and Support
If a new hire lacks critical equipment, access, or training, the manager should escalate the issue to IT or HR within 1 business day.
Managers remain accountable for resolving onboarding blockers quickly so the employee can become productive on schedule.
