# New Hire Onboarding Guide

## First Week Expectations
New employees complete orientation, receive equipment, and review core policies during their first week.
Managers are expected to schedule a team introduction meeting and a 30-day onboarding plan by the employee's second working day.

## Required Setup
IT provisions email, single sign-on, and collaboration tools before the employee's start date whenever possible.
Employees must complete multi-factor authentication setup and confirm access to all role-critical systems within the first 3 business days.

## Introductory Check-Ins
Managers hold formal onboarding check-ins at 30, 60, and 90 days.
These check-ins cover role clarity, training progress, blockers, and any support the employee needs to become fully productive.
