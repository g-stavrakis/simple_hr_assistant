# Flexible Benefits FAQ

## Enrollment Window
Employees may enroll in or change flexible benefit selections during the annual open enrollment period.
Changes outside open enrollment are only allowed after a qualifying life event, such as marriage, birth, or loss of other coverage.

## Dependent Eligibility
Eligible dependents may be added to medical, dental, and vision coverage according to plan rules.
Employees must provide supporting documentation within 30 days of the requested change.

## Benefits Support
Questions about plan coverage, payroll deductions, or enrollment issues should be submitted to the People Operations team.
Complex claims issues may be escalated to the benefits broker for plan-specific guidance.
