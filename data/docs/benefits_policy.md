# Employee Benefits Guide

## Health Insurance
Full-time employees are eligible for company-sponsored health insurance starting on the first day of the month following their start date.
Coverage includes medical, dental, and vision plans for employees, with dependent coverage available at the employee's cost.

## Wellness Stipend
Employees receive a wellness stipend of 500 EUR per calendar year.
Eligible expenses include gym memberships, fitness classes, meditation apps, and ergonomic home office equipment.

## Learning Budget
Each employee may request up to 1,200 EUR per year for approved professional development.
Covered expenses may include online courses, certifications, books, and conference registration fees.
