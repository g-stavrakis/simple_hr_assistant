# Performance Review Policy

## Review Cycle
The company runs formal performance reviews twice per year, in June and December.
Managers must submit written evaluations by the published HR deadline so calibration meetings can be completed on time.

## Feedback Expectations
Employees should receive regular feedback throughout the year and should not learn about major performance concerns for the first time during a formal review.
Managers are expected to document goals, outcomes, and development themes in the performance system.

## Performance Improvement Support
If sustained performance concerns are identified, managers must partner with HR on a documented support plan.
The plan should define expected improvements, check-in dates, available coaching, and the period for reassessment.
