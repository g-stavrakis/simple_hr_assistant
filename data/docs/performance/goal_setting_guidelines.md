# Goal Setting Guidelines

## Goal Quality
Employees and managers should set goals that are specific, measurable, and aligned with team or company priorities.
Goals should describe expected outcomes, not just activity, and should include a target timeline where appropriate.

## Mid-Cycle Adjustments
Goals may be updated during the review cycle when business priorities materially change.
Any significant goal changes should be documented and discussed in a one-on-one meeting.

## Shared Accountability
Employees are responsible for tracking progress and surfacing blockers early.
Managers are responsible for providing context, feedback, and support needed to make goal attainment realistic.
