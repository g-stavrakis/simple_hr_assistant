# Travel Expense Examples

## Reimbursable Examples
Reasonable airfare, hotel charges, local transportation, baggage fees, and business meals during approved travel are generally reimbursable.
Internet access needed for work while traveling is also reimbursable when not already included in lodging.

## Non-Reimbursable Examples
Personal entertainment, minibar purchases, leisure travel extensions, traffic fines, and expenses for non-employees are not reimbursable unless explicitly approved.
Room upgrades for personal preference are not covered by the company.

## Documentation Standards
Employees should include business purpose, dates, attendees when relevant, and itemized receipts for each submitted claim.
Incomplete submissions may be returned for correction before approval.
